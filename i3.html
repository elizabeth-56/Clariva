CLARIVA

Clarity of Words

Dyslexia-friendly reading assistant with intelligent formatting, audio support, pronunciation guidance, and smart highlighting.

Text Formatting

The text formatting feature enables users to personalize font style, spacing, and color to create a comfortable and dyslexia-friendly reading environment. This adaptive layout reduces visual stress and improves readability.

Text-to-Speech

The text-to-speech feature allows users to listen to words either by long-pressing individual text or by using a read-aloud option for continuous audio support. This enhances comprehension, reinforces word recognition, and supports multisensory learning.

Speech-to-Text

The speech-to-text feature evaluates the user’s pronunciation by converting spoken words into text and instantly providing feedback through a pop-up. This interactive correction mechanism helps users practice accurate pronunciation and build reading confidence.

Text Highlighting

The first letter is made bold along with highlighting vowels and confusing letter pairs.
